Free and Easy

    Rely on reducing, resuing, and repurposing before you recycle! Most recycling programs in the U.S. aren't actually doing good for the planet

    Carry reusable straws with you when you're out

    Wash your cloths with cool or cold water

    Unplug cords from your outlets whenever you're not using an item

    Unsubscribe from emails you don't need

    Take shorter showers and turn the temperature down

    Carry a reusable water bottle where ever you go

    Refuse plastic whenever you're able to

    Bring reusable bags with you to the grocery store, or any store for that matter

    Don't buy glitter or confetti

    Bottle brick your unrecyclable items

    Stay away from products that contain microbeads

    Don't purchase from companies that use fast-fashion

    Sign petitions and share them with your family and friends
