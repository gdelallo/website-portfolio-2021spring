Free and Easy Ways to be a Greener Person

  • Carry reusable straws with you when you're out

  • Wash your cloths with cool or cold water when possible

  • Unsubscribe from emails you don't need (they're pumping CO2 into the air!)

  • Take shorter showers(I know it sucks, but water conservation is important)

  • Carry a reusable water bottle where ever you go

  • Refuse plastic whenever you can

  • Bring reusable bags with you to any and every store (here's my go-to)

  • Avoid glitter and confetti (it's made of plastic...ew)

  • Rely on reducing, resuing, and repurposing before you recycle! Most recycling programs in the U.S. aren't actually doing good for the planet

  • Bottle brick your unrecyclable items

  • Stay away from products that contain microbeads

  • Get in the know about mirco plastics and how to reduce your impact

  • Don't purchase from companies that use fast-fashion

  • Sign petitions and share them with your friends and family
