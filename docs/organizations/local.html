Local (Pittsburgh/Pennsylvania) National International

Getting involved with your local grassroots organizations is the best way to make a direct impact. Working within your community is not only effective in locally combating climate issues and reducing pollution, but also in rasising awareness about climate change and environmental issues.

Local Organizations















  • Allegheny Defense Project

    • “Working for the protection of the natural heritage of the Alleghenies.”


  • Sierra Club: Allegheny Chapter

    • “We are one of nine regional groups across the state that help the people of Pennsylvania explore, enjoy, and protect our state's many environmental resources and fight for safe and livable communities.”





  • 350 Pittsburgh

    • “Join the ever growing number of groups in Southwestern PA whose work includes preventing, mitigating, or adapting to climate change.”



  • Allegheny Land Trust

    • “The premier non-profit, environmental education organization in southwestern Pennsylvania.”


  • Land Trust Alliance

    • “Together, conserving the places you love.”


  • Clean Air Council

    • “Protecting Everyone's Right to Breathe Clean Air.”


  • The Nature Conservancy

    • “Advocates for sound environmental and conservation policies, works to elect environmentally friendly candidates to public office, and then holds them to their promises.”




Find these organizations and more at http://www.eco-usa.net/orgs/pa.shtml
